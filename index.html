Hi! I'm Andrew

I am an adventurer and landscape photographer based in Seattle, specializing in capturing mountainous landscapes.

I grew up in the flatlands of Missouri and was fortunate to have the opportunity to visit places like Colorado, Wyoming, and Alaska as a kid.

I gained a special appreciation for mountains through these trips. It was, and still is, the unique blend of beauty and danger that draws me to them.

I wish to continue to explore and capture the untouched beauty of mountains around the world with the goal to inspire others, and to show those who do not have the means of seeing for their own eyes, the treasures that this world has to offer.
